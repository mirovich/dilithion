Why Dilithion exists

Most major cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin and Ethereum, rely on elliptic curve cryptography that quantum computers will eventually break. Dilithion uses NIST-standard post-quantum signatures from day one.

01

Quantum-Resistant Signatures

CRYSTALS-Dilithium, the NIST post-quantum standard. Your keys are safe even when quantum computers break ECDSA.

02

Fair CPU Mining

RandomX proof-of-work with DFMP ensures one miner, one vote. No ASICs, no GPU farms. Mine with the computer you already own.

03

Dual-Chain Architecture

DIL for store of value. DilV for fast payments. Two chains, one ecosystem — bridged to Base for DeFi access.
